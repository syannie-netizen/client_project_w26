Creating Your List of 40 Organizations

Guidelines to identify organizations that align with your goals, interests, and opportunities.

10 Dream Organizations

Start with 10 organizations you have dreamed about or are curious about. Consider adding their competitors as well. Think about companies in alignment with your values and identities. Examples include:

  • Human Rights Campaign Corporate Equality Index
  • 50+ Black-owned and led companies
  • Corporate Religious Equity, Diversity & Inclusion (REDI) Index
  • Certified B Corporations

If you’re stuck, reflect on your hobbies and interests. For example:

  • Music lovers: search top music streaming companies
  • Travel enthusiasts: find a list of travel sites and apps

10 Alumni Organizations

Focus on organizations that have hired U-M/UMSI alumni. Useful sources include:

  • UMSI Employment & Internship Reports
  • UCAN
  • LinkedIn

International students can also refer to the International Alumni Outcomes spreadsheet. Think beyond just U-M alumni to other affiliations, associations, and communities where you have connections.

10 Organizations with Job Postings

Use job sites like Indeed to find 10 organizations with current openings. Use the “WHAT” field (job title or function) and “WHERE” field (location) to tailor searches.

Example: Enter “Data Engineer” in the WHAT field, and “Seattle” in the WHERE field.

International students can use tools like the Ultimate H1B Sponsor Checker Chrome Extension to identify potential sponsors.

10 Trending or Open to Sponsorship Organizations

To find the final 10 organizations, search online for trending organizations or fields of interest using keywords like “trends.” Skim articles to identify organizations and add them to your list.

Examples of searches:

  • Machine Learning Trends
  • Largest Libraries in the U.S.
  • Health Data Trends
  • Fastest Growing Companies in Seattle
  • Wealthiest Cultural Institutes
  • Beauty UX Trends

International students may prioritize organizations with a history of sponsorship using sites like Interstride or MyVisaJobs.
